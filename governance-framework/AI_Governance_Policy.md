# AI Governance Policy

## Purpose

This policy establishes governance standards for the development, deployment, and monitoring of Artificial Intelligence systems.

---

## Governance Structure

### Board Oversight

Strategic oversight of AI initiatives.

### Executive Leadership

Accountable for implementation.

### Technical Teams

Responsible for development and monitoring.

### Compliance Teams

Responsible for auditing and regulatory alignment.

---

## Monitoring

All AI systems must undergo periodic review.

---

## Reporting

Critical incidents must be reported immediately.
