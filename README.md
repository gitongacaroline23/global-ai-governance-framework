# Global AI Governance Framework

## Overview

The Global AI Governance Framework is an enterprise-grade initiative designed to provide organizations, governments, and technology leaders with practical frameworks for responsible artificial intelligence governance.

This repository focuses on:

- Responsible AI
- AI Risk Management
- AI Ethics
- AI Compliance
- AI Auditing
- AI Governance Strategy
- Human Oversight Systems

---

## Objectives

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries worldwide. Organizations require structured governance systems that ensure AI solutions remain:

- Fair
- Transparent
- Secure
- Explainable
- Accountable

This framework provides guidance for implementing AI systems safely and responsibly.

---

## Key Components

### AI Governance

Governance policies and procedures for AI systems.

### Risk Management

Identification and mitigation of AI-related risks.

### Compliance

Alignment with regulatory standards and organizational policies.

### Ethics

Frameworks promoting fairness, transparency, and accountability.

### Human Oversight

Processes ensuring appropriate human involvement in AI decision-making.
