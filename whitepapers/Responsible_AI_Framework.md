# Responsible AI Framework

## Executive Summary

Artificial Intelligence is transforming industries worldwide.

Organizations deploying AI systems must establish governance frameworks that ensure accountability, fairness, transparency, security, and compliance.

This document presents a Responsible AI Framework designed for enterprise environments.

---

## Principles

### Transparency

AI systems should provide explainable outputs.

### Fairness

Bias detection and mitigation should be mandatory.

### Accountability

Organizations remain responsible for AI decisions.

### Privacy

Sensitive data must be protected.

### Human Oversight

Humans remain involved in critical decisions.

---

## Implementation Strategy

1. Establish Governance Committee
2. Define AI Policies
3. Conduct Risk Assessments
4. Audit AI Systems
5. Implement Monitoring

---

## Conclusion

Responsible AI is critical for sustainable and trustworthy innovation.
